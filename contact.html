Get in Touch

Welcome to our contact us page. At Solidarity Child Rescue Center, we value the communication and feedback from our visitors and supporters. Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and provide a safe and loving environment for children in need.
If you have any questions, suggestions, or would like to get involved in our mission, please do not hesitate to contact us. We are committed to ensuring that every child has access to basic necessities, education, healthcare, and most importantly, love. Your support is critical to achieving our goals and making a positive impact in the lives of these children. We look forward to hearing from you.

Our Location

We are located in the neighbourhood of Kasarani 10 mins drive from the northeast of Nairobi's central business district. We are easily accessible by public transportation or car.
Use the interactive map below to find your way. Our doors are always open to visitors and supporters. We would love to hear from you and have you join us in our mission to help children in need.
