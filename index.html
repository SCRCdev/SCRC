Solidarity Child Rescue Centre...

We are a small organization with a big heart, concerned with the plight of the street children, orphaned and vulnerable children in Kenya.

Who we are

Our programmes...

We focus on providing these children with basic needs with a particular emphasis on education. Our aim is to support each needy child so that they can be stable and happy and also to reach a stage where they are self-sufficient. We aim to provide a safe and secure environment for the children to grow up in.
