Who we are

Solidarity Child Rescue Centre is a small Christian centre located on our own half acre plot in Kasarani, Nairobi, the capital city of Kenya. It was started as a residential children's home in May 1996 by a few concerned christians to address the problem of orphans, streetkids and vulnerable children within the city. We obtained official registration as a charitable society in September 1997 and we are also registered as a charitable children's institution(CCI) with the Children's department. We also have a registered primary school and church on the same premises.

Our Mission

We seek to help destitute children and families mostly around Nairobi who for various reasons of poverty, family breakdown, abuse, neglect, or refugee status and those living in situations of suffering. Our residential children are mostly orphaned, totally abandoned or have been referred to us due to abuse. The boys and girls under our care range in age from six to eighteen years of age.
